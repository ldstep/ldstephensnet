As someone who goes to great lengths to avoid advertising, using uBlock Origin on my MacBook and Wipr on my iPhone I rarely see a single ad. I've almost forgotten what the cluttered version of the web even looks like.

That's why I'm rethinking how I'll use ChatGPT. Instead of the dedicated ChatGPT app, where I have little control over the content, I plan to use the browser version. My logic is: by staying within the browser, I can let my ad blocker keep the interface clean.

I'll be watching to see how intrusive these ads actually become.
